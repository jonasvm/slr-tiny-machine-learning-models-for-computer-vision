## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main Objective:**  
To review deep learning approaches used for human detection in UAV imagery and analyze their performance, datasets, and research trends.

**Problem Addressed:**  
Human detection from UAV imagery is difficult due to factors such as small object size, occlusions, complex backgrounds, motion blur, and variations in altitude and camera angles. The review aims to consolidate existing research and identify key challenges and directions for future work.

**Scope:**  
- UAV imagery  
- Human detection  
- Deep learning  
- Aerial computer vision

**Main Contributions Claimed by the Authors:**  
- Review of deep learning methods for UAV-based human detection  
- Analysis of datasets used for aerial human detection  
- Comparison of deep learning detection architectures  
- Identification of open challenges and research directions
