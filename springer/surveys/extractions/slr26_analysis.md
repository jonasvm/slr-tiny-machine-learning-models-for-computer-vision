## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main objective of the review:**  
To analyze and summarize research on human detection in UAV imagery using deep learning, focusing on detection models, datasets, evaluation metrics, and application contexts.

**Problem the review claims to solve:**  
Human detection in UAV imagery is challenging due to factors such as small object size, complex backgrounds, varying camera angles, and resolution limitations. The review aims to consolidate the current research landscape and highlight the main approaches and challenges.

**Scope:**  
- UAV imagery  
- Human detection  
- Deep learning object detection  
- Computer vision for aerial surveillance  
- Small object detection

**Main contributions claimed by the authors:**

- Compilation and discussion of research studies on human detection using UAV imagery.
- Identification and comparison of deep learning architectures used for detection.
- Survey of datasets containing annotated human instances in UAV imagery.
- Discussion of evaluation metrics used in the literature.
- Identification of open challenges and future research directions.
