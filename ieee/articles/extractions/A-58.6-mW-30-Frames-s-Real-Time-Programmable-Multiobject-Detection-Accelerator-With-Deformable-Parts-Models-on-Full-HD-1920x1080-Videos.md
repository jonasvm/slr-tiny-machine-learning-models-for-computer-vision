Results

Summary: The accelerator achieves Full HD 1920×1080 object detection at 30 fps with 58.6 mW and 0.94 nJ/pixel. On PASCAL VOC 2007, it reports mean AP 0.26, matching Fast DPM but below original DPM v5.

Limitations

The paper notes that accuracy error could potentially be reduced by retraining parts and deformation filters with vector-quantized features.
