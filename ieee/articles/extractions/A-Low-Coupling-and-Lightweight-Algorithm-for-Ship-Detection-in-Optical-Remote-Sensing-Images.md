Results

Summary: LCNet achieved 95.27% mAP(12), 88.91% F1-score, and 69.7 FPS on HRSC2016. The proposed compression method achieved at least 80% compression with negligible performance loss.

Limitations

LCNet speed is slightly lower than YOLOF and YOLOX-M due to large feature maps generated by the deconvolution layer; future work aims to extend the method to other datasets and object detection tasks.
