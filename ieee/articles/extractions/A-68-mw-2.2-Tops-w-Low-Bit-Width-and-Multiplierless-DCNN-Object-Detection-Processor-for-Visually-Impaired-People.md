Results

Summary: Compressed YOLO V2 tiny achieved 59.6% mAP on VOC 2007 + 2012 with 1.8% accuracy loss compared to a fixed 16-bit model. The fabricated 55 nm processor achieved 68 mW power, 155.4 GOPS throughput, 2.2 TOPS/W energy efficiency, and 36 ms latency for YOLO V2 tiny.
