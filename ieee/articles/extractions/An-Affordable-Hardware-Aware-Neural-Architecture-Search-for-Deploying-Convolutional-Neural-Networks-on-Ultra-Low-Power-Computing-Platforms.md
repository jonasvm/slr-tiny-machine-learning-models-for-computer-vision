### Results

* Summary: The proposed HW-NAS generated tiny CNNs that fit ultra-low-power STM32 MCU constraints while achieving up to 77% accuracy on VWW, 72.6% on CIFAR-10, and 91.8% on Melanoma Skin Cancer. The method reported runtime RAM as low as 15.5 KiB, Flash as low as 8.6 KiB, and latency between 29.7 ms and 119.3 ms.

### Limitations

* Large datasets may be problematic when the NAS procedure runs on embedded systems.
* The proposed HW-NAS is designed for tiny CNNs and may not achieve state-of-the-art results on high-performance MCUs.
