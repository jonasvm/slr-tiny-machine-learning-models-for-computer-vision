## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main objective of the review**

The paper aims to review the state-of-the-art technologies enabling the Metaverse, focusing on the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and 6G communication technologies and their role in supporting immersive virtual environments.

**Problem the review claims to solve**

The authors aim to understand how the convergence of AI and next-generation wireless communication (B5G/6G) can enable scalable, immersive, and sustainable Metaverse environments.

**Scope**

The review covers several technologies associated with the Metaverse ecosystem, including:

- Artificial Intelligence
- 6G wireless communication
- Edge AI
- XR technologies (VR, AR, MR)
- Spatial computing
- IoT/IoE
- Mobile edge computing
- Blockchain
- Digital twins

**Main contributions claimed by the authors**

The paper claims the following contributions:

- Explains the underlying technologies enabling the Metaverse, including VR, MR, AR, and spatial computing.
- Reviews the state-of-the-art of AI technologies and their role in supporting Metaverse applications.
- Analyzes the role of B5G/6G communication technologies in enabling immersive experiences.
- Examines the integration of AI and 6G for Metaverse infrastructure.
- Discusses applications, ongoing projects, sustainability aspects, challenges, and future research directions.
