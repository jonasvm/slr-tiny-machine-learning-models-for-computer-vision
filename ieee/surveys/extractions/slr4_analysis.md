## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main objective of the review**

To analyze the evolution of YOLO object detection architectures from YOLOv1 to YOLOv8, focusing on architectural innovations, training strategies, benchmark performance, and real-world applications.

**Problem the review claims to solve**

Previous reviews often focus either on:

- specific YOLO variants, or  
- specific application domains.

This review aims to provide a **comprehensive analysis of mainstream YOLO variants from YOLOv1 to YOLOv8**, including architectural changes and application domains.

**Scope**

- Computer vision
- Object detection
- YOLO architectures
- Edge computing and industrial applications

**Main contributions claimed by the authors**

1. Detailed analysis of the architectural evolution of YOLO variants.
2. Examination of training strategies used across different YOLO versions.
3. Comparative benchmarking of YOLO variants using performance metrics.
4. Overview of real-world applications across multiple domains.
5. Discussion of challenges and future research directions.

- discussion of challenges and future research directions
