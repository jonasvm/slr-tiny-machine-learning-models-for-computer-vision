## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main Objective:**  
To provide a comprehensive overview of on-device AI models, including their applications, technical challenges, optimization methods, and future development trends in edge computing environments.

**Problem Addressed:**  
The increasing deployment of AI models on edge devices introduces challenges related to limited computational resources, energy consumption, latency requirements, and privacy concerns. The review aims to synthesize existing research addressing these issues.

**Scope:**  

- On-device AI models  
- Edge AI / Edge intelligence  
- AI deployment on edge and IoT devices  
- Model optimization and compression  
- Hardware acceleration  
- Resource-constrained AI environments  

**Main Contributions Claimed by the Authors:**

- Provides a comprehensive overview of on-device AI models.
- Identifies key technical challenges for deploying AI models on edge devices.
- Reviews optimization techniques including data optimization, model compression, and hardware acceleration.
- Discusses emerging trends and future research directions in on-device AI.
- Highlights practical implications for real-world deployment.
