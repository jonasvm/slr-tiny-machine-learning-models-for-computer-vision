Database: ACM Digital Library

Search query:

("tinyml" OR "tiny machine learning" OR "edge ai" OR "embedded ai" OR "microcontroller" OR "mcu")  
AND  
("computer vision" OR "image classification" OR "object detection" OR "image segmentation" OR "object tracking")

Filters applied:

- Content type: Journals
- Publication years: 2015–2025

Total results returned:

325 papers

Selection criteria applied during screening:

**Inclusion criteria:**

- Papers related to TinyML, Edge AI, Embedded AI, or embedded/on-device AI
- Papers focused on computer vision tasks
- Papers involving image classification, object detection, segmentation, object tracking, facial recognition, visual recognition, or video-based perception
- Peer-reviewed journal publications

**Exclusion criteria:**

- Papers not related to computer vision
- Papers focused mainly on non-visual modalities, such as audio, RF sensing, wearable sensors, biomedical signals, NLP, or networking-only data
- Papers with strong cloud, edge-cloud, 5G, networking, or distributed-system dependence and no clear TinyML/on-device focus
- Papers not clearly related to embedded, constrained, low-power, or edge AI systems
- Broad AI, IoT, security, hardware, or systems papers without a clear computer vision and constrained-device focus

**Notes**

Title-based screening was used for this initial classification.  
Papers marked as Keep should be checked in full text or abstract screening.  
Papers marked as Discard are considered outside the scope of TinyML / Edge AI for computer vision.
