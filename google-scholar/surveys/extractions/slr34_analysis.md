# 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main objective of the review**

The objective of the paper is to provide a comprehensive overview of TinyML, focusing on its applications, enabling tools and frameworks, hardware platforms, limitations, and future research directions in IoT and embedded systems environments.

**Problem the review claims to solve**

Traditional machine learning and deep learning models require substantial computational power, memory, and energy, which makes their deployment difficult on resource-constrained IoT and embedded devices. The review investigates how TinyML enables the deployment of machine learning models on such devices and discusses the associated challenges and opportunities.

**Scope**

The review focuses on:

- Tiny Machine Learning (TinyML)
- Embedded systems
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Edge AI
- Resource-constrained devices
- Applications of TinyML across multiple domains

**Main contributions claimed by the authors**

The authors claim the following contributions:

- Provide a comprehensive overview of TinyML principles and background
- Explore real-world applications of TinyML in domains such as healthcare, agriculture, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation
- Analyze tools, frameworks, and development environments used in TinyML
- Identify challenges and limitations related to deployment on edge devices
- Present future research directions and emerging opportunities in TinyML
