## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main Objective of the Review**

The paper aims to provide a comprehensive overview of Embedded Artificial Intelligence (EAI), covering its conceptual foundations, hardware platforms, software frameworks, lightweight neural network models, deployment pipelines, applications, and open research challenges.

**Problem the Review Claims to Solve**

The authors attempt to consolidate the fragmented research landscape of Embedded AI and provide a structured understanding of the ecosystem required to deploy artificial intelligence models on embedded and resource-constrained devices.

**Scope**

Embedded Artificial Intelligence, including:

- Embedded machine learning
- Edge AI systems
- Lightweight neural network architectures
- Hardware accelerators
- AI deployment frameworks
- Embedded AI applications

Application domains discussed include:

- Autonomous driving
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Smart devices
- Industrial systems
- Healthcare devices
- Wearables

**Main Contributions Claimed by the Authors**

- Formal definition and conceptualization of Embedded AI
- Overview of the Embedded AI technology stack
- Survey of hardware platforms supporting AI on embedded systems
- Review of software frameworks and development toolchains
- Discussion of lightweight deep learning models
- Explanation of AI deployment pipelines on embedded devices
- Analysis of application domains and emerging research challenges
