## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main Objective:**  
To provide a comprehensive survey of TinyML technologies, applications, and challenges, with a focus on identifying key application sectors and research trends.

**Problem Addressed:**  
Existing surveys either focus on specific technical aspects of TinyML or isolated domains, lacking a unified taxonomy of TinyML applications across major sectors.

**Scope:**  
TinyML and embedded machine learning in resource-constrained devices, particularly in:

- Healthcare
- Environment
- Smart farming
- Anomaly detection
- Edge intelligence and IoT systems

**Main Contributions Claimed by the Authors:**

- Definition and overview of TinyML
- Taxonomy of TinyML applications
- Survey of TinyML solutions in critical sectors
- Identification of challenges and open research questions
- Discussion of future research directions
