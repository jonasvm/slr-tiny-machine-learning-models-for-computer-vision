## 1. Basic Information

**Authors:**  
Hui Han, Julien Siebert

**Year:**  
2022

**Journal / Conference:**  
2022 International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Information and Communication (ICAIIC)

**Type of Review:**  
Systematic Literature Review with data synthesis

---

## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main objective of the review:**  
To synthesize the existing body of research on TinyML and provide a structured overview of the emerging field.

**Problem the review claims to solve:**  
TinyML research is fragmented and still in an early stage, lacking comprehensive synthesis that integrates findings and clarifies relationships across the literature.

**Scope:**  
TinyML research focusing on the intersection of embedded systems and machine learning, particularly ML inference on microcontrollers and ultra-low-power edge devices.

**Main contributions claimed by the authors:**

- Systematic synthesis of TinyML literature since 2019
- Organization of TinyML research into five conceptual elements:
  - hardware
  - framework
  - datasets
  - use cases
  - algorithms/models
- A roadmap to support researchers and practitioners exploring the TinyML field
