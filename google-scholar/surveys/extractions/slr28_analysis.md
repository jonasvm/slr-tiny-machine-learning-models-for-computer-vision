## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main objective of the review**

Provide a comprehensive overview of edge deep learning, focusing on the deployment of deep learning models on edge devices for computer vision applications, particularly in medical diagnostics.

**Problem the review claims to solve**

The review aims to clarify the current state of the art in deploying deep learning models on resource-constrained edge devices while maintaining real-time performance, efficiency, and reliability, especially in applications involving computer vision and healthcare.

**Scope**

- Edge computing
- Deep learning
- Computer vision
- Medical diagnostics
- Lightweight neural networks
- Model compression techniques
- Edge hardware platforms

**Main contributions claimed by the authors**

- Overview of the fundamental concepts of edge computing and deep learning
- Categorization of edge hardware platforms
- Discussion of lightweight neural network design and model compression methods
- Review of edge deep learning applications in computer vision
- Analysis of applications in medical diagnostics
- Identification of research challenges and future directions
