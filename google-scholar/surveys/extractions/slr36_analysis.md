## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main Objective:**  
To provide a comprehensive overview of TinyML technology, including neural network compression techniques, TinyML hardware platforms, development boards, software frameworks, applications, educational resources, and future research directions.

**Problem Addressed:**  
TinyML is a rapidly emerging field combining embedded systems and machine learning, with increasing research output and technological complexity. The review aims to organize and classify the technologies and approaches involved in TinyML to provide a clearer understanding of the field.

**Scope:**  
- TinyML  
- Embedded machine learning  
- Edge AI on microcontrollers  
- Neural network compression techniques  
- TinyML hardware platforms  
- TinyML software frameworks  
- TinyML applications

**Main Contributions Claimed by the Authors:**

- Classification of neural network compression and optimization techniques.
- Review of TinyML software frameworks and libraries.
- Review of TinyML hardware platforms and development boards.
- Categorization of TinyML applications.
- Compilation of educational resources related to TinyML.
- Discussion of future directions and challenges for TinyML technology.
