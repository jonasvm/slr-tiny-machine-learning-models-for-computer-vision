## 2. Scope and Purpose of the Review

**Main objective of the review**

The paper aims to provide a comprehensive overview of lightweight deep learning techniques designed for deployment in resource-constrained environments such as embedded systems, mobile devices, and edge platforms.

**Problem the review claims to solve**

Deep neural networks typically require high computational power, memory capacity, and energy consumption, which limits their deployment on embedded and edge devices. The review investigates methods that enable efficient and lightweight deep learning models suitable for such constrained environments.

**Scope**

The review focuses on:

- Lightweight deep learning
- Edge AI
- Embedded AI
- Resource-constrained computing environments
- Efficient neural network architectures
- Model compression techniques

Application domains discussed include:

- computer vision
- mobile AI
- embedded systems
- edge intelligence

**Main contributions claimed by the authors**

- A comprehensive survey of lightweight deep learning methods.
- A taxonomy of model compression and efficiency techniques.
- Analysis of efficient neural network architectures.
- Discussion of hardware-aware model design.
- Identification of future research directions in lightweight AI systems.
